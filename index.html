    Hello!

    SITE UNDER CONSTRUCTION!!

    I am a Postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering Northwestern University, Evanston Illinois. I work in the Kamomoa Lab under the supervision of Professor Josiah Hester. I received my PhD in May 2020 from Lahore Univesity of Management Science LUMS Pakistan under the supervision of Professor Muhammad Hamad Alizai. My thesis was Fast and Energy-efficient Intermittent Computing.

    My research work focuses on providing system support for battery-less embedded devices, which will serve as a basic block in building a sustainable future for computing. I am also interested in systems that enable novices and hobbyists to develop applications for battery-less devices. In addition, I am broadly interested in wearable computing, mobile health, and low-power wireless sensors.
