I am a Postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering NorthWestern University, Evanston Illinois. I work in the Kamomoa Lab under the supervision of Professor Josiah Hester. I received my PhD in May 2020 from Lahore Univesity of Management Science LUMS PAkistan under the supervision of Dr. Hamad Alizai.

My research work focuses on providing system support for battery-less embedded devices, which will serve as a basic block in building a sustainable future for computing. I am also interested in systems that enable novices and hobbyists to develop applications for battery-less devices. In addition, I am broadly interested in wearable computing, mobile health, and low-power wireless sensors. MS from Lahore University of Management Sciences, Pakistan.
